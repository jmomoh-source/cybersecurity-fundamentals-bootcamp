# Launching Your Cybersecurity Journey

---

## 🌐 What is Cybersecurity?
- Protection of computer systems and networks from malicious actors.  
- Prevents unauthorized disclosure, theft, damage, disruption, or misdirection of services.  

---

## ⚙️ Foundations
- **Core Principle:** “Cybersecurity is evolving and 50% networking.”  
- You cannot secure what you don’t understand — networking knowledge is essential.  

---

## 🔑 Aspects of Cybersecurity
- **Blue Teaming** — Defensive security.  
- **Red Teaming** — Offensive security.  
- **Purple Teaming** — Collaboration between red and blue.  
- **Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC).**  

---

## 📚 Fundamental Requirement
- Master the fundamentals before specializing.  
- Cybersecurity domains are interwoven — strong foundation is critical.  

---

## 🧭 Career Landscape
- Covers major careers but not exhaustive.  
- Sub-domains include:
  - **Advanced Threat Protection** — malware analysis, sandboxing, analytics.  
  - **Network Security** — firewalls, NAC, IDS/IPS, penetration testing.  
  - **Data Security** — encryption, leakage prevention.  
  - **Risk Governance & Compliance** — ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI, audits.  
  - **Infrastructure Security** — DNS, mail, SIEM, zero-day tracking.  
  - **Mobile Security** — rogue AP detection, OWASP Mobile Top 10.  
  - **Application Security** — secure coding, app scanning, penetration testing.  
  - **System Security** — patch management, vulnerability scanning, OWASP Top 10, SANS CWE Top 25.  

---

## 🔵 Blue Teaming Roles
- SOC Analysts, Incident Responders, Digital Forensics Analysts.  
- Threat Hunters, CTI Analysts, Malware Analysts.  
- Network Analysts, OSINT specialists, Vulnerability Management.  

**Essential Knowledge:** Threat modeling, vulnerability assessment, IAM, network analysis, standards/frameworks, OS fundamentals.  

---

## 🔴 Offensive Security Roles
- Penetration Testers, Ethical Hackers, Offensive Security Engineers.  
- Red Team Operators, Exploit Developers.  

**Role Comparison:**  
- Penetration Testers → test networks/apps with permission.  
- Red Team Operators → emulate adversaries, use social engineering, physical breaches.  

**Essential Knowledge:** Networking, OS, virtualization, CLI, scripting, programming, attack frameworks, OWASP.  

**Certifications:** Security+, Network+, eJPT, OSCP, CEH.  

---

## 🎓 General & Specialized Roles
- **Generalist:** Cybersecurity Analyst (works across offensive/defensive).  
- **Specialized:** Cloud Security, Security Engineer, Architect, Cryptographer, DevSecOps, Application Security Engineer.  

**Certifications:** CompTIA Sec+/Net+, Cisco CyberOps, CISSP, ISC2 CC, eJPT, OSCP.  
**Microsoft Pathways:** SC-900, AZ-900.  

---

## 📊 Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)
- **Roles:** InfoSec Officer, GRC Analyst, Risk Analyst.  
- **Focus:** Frameworks, standards, policies, compliance.  
- **Responsibilities:**  
  - Compliance → identify requirements, manage policies, monitor adherence.  
  - Risk Mitigation → assess threats, implement controls.  

---

## 📚 Learning Resources
- TryHackMe, HackTheBox, letsdefend.io, YouTube, SANS.  

---

## ✅ Key Takeaways
- Cybersecurity is broad: defensive, offensive, collaborative, and compliance.  
- Networking knowledge is foundational.  
- Careers span SOC, penetration testing, GRC, DevSecOps, and more.  
- Certifications (CompTIA, OSCP, CISSP, Microsoft) provide structured pathways.  
- Continuous learning via platforms like TryHackMe and HackTheBox is essential.
